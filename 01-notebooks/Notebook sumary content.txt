0.0-data-preparation.ipynb
- This notebook contains all the exploration, cleaning, and preparation steps of the accident data.

0.1-accident-general-evaluation.ipynb
- This notebook contains the evaluation of accidents over different time intervals.

0.2-accident-police-evaluation.ipynb
- This notebook contains the evaluation of the impact of different accident factors in the occurrence of drivers and passengers.

- This notebook contains a brief evaluation of traffic in Lisbon as well as the generation of a dataframe that intersects the traffic data with accident data.
